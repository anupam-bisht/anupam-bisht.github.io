In some of my free time during vacations or otherwise, I find some time to volunteer. This page gives a glimpse of some of the activities.

Calgary Optics & Photonics Student Society. (COPSS)

October 18th 2019: Eastbrook Elementary School, Newell County, AB, Canada
Contributed as a speaker at 2 workshops for grades 4 and 5. We reached over 140 students through this workshop. The workshop consists of various simple live demonstrations to explain optics and light.

Volunteering at Champawat, Uttarakhand (India)

Himalaya Water Service Tatha Vikas Avam Paryavaran Sanrakshan Samiti (HIMWATS) is a Non-profit organization which is aimed to provide education in rural schools in the Uttarakhand region, India. I have devoted some of my time during vacations (2015-2019) to help in the organization by teaching school children certain topics, and contributing in the following activities.

A document consisting of suggestions to implement Tablet assisted Teaching at Government Primary schools in rural areas in Uttarakhand. Here is a report on it: REPORT

I also helped the organization to shoot experimental demo videos in Hindi language. Some videos are Identification of a Magnet, To find Acid or Base and Surface Tension.
